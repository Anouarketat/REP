Email & Messaging

Are you looking at setting up an email server for your company, branch office, institutions, or any size of organizations ?

We always have the right solutions to meet your business objectives! Typically, there are two categories of messaging solutions for choice:

On-premises Enterprise Messaging Solution

On-premises version means a corporate client purchases Microsoft Exchange Server and Client Access License (CAL) and installs the software system into the servers in the corporate network. Microsoft has revamped the architecture to provide enterprise client with a feature-rich, secure, reliable and robust messaging and collaboration solution. The software is a one-off investment unless one pays for Microsoft Software Assurance (SA) subscription for future version protection.

Cloud-based Enterprise Messaging Solution

If a corporation wants to save the hassle of setting up and maintaining the complicated Microsoft Exchange Server, the hosted cloud-based Microsoft Exchange Online service is the choice ! A company can subscribe to Microsoft Exchange Online, a product in the Microsoft 365 family, in the a la carte or Microsoft 365 bundled products form. The annual subscription bases on the number of mailboxes.
